Building Process

Step 1: Designing the PCB & Soldering

If you have previous knowledge of PCB designing, just download the rar file, unzip, and then follow the schematic. I have used EasyEDA software for designing.

Schematics.rar

Watch this video for learning PCB designing in EasyEDA.

Otherwise, you can connect the components using Veroboard like the image below. I only developed the motor driver on it.

I also added a picture of the PCB version of the hardware.

Step 2: Designing the Frame

Frame photo is given below. It is best practice to make it with 3D designing. I will attach the 3D design file below. You can also make one with some light weight material.

Step 3: Connecting the Motor

Finally you need to connect all the component as the picture below. You should maintain the rotation direction of the motor and the propeller as the picture below. Also make sure to place the mpu6050 in same direction like given in the picture. CW means clockwise and ACW means anticlockwise.

Step 4: Uploading the Code

Now download the rar file, unzip it, upload the Arduino code to the esp32 and install the apk to your phone.

codeandapk.rar

After uploading the code, it will take some time for calibration. In that time, Red led will blink in two pattern. Donâ€™t move or vibrate the drone at that time. While the calibration is over green led will become on. Now you need to open your phone and turn on the Bluetooth. Then you may find some Bluetooth list like below.

Select ESP32_BT to pair.

Then open the app and move the two balls in any direction and leave the balls so that P,R,Y become zero.

Then move the left joystick ball in the bottom so that that T also becomes zero.

Now click connect & You will see the paired list. Select the one like below.

You will be confirmed by written connected in green in the top.

Now connection is complete. For staring the motors just move left joystick ball to bottom left. Green light will become off in the drone indicating it is ready to fly. Now you can operate this remote as drone remote. Right joystick is for left-right, front-back movement and left joystick is for up-down and yaw movement. For shutting down simply move the left joystick ball bottom right.

Troubleshooting

If in the setup red led is blinking twice periodically then it is saying the microcontroller cannot communicate with the mpu9250. So, you may need to troubleshoot the connection or the sensor. In addition, there may be error during quadcopter is flying. The chart shows relation of blink and error

Future Updates

This drone is not perfect as you can see in the video. The microcontroller gets reset every time while I start the motor. So, right now I am giving external power to the microcontroller from USB. But it is not a solution. The guy from the last video says it is due to EMI of the motor driver to the sensor. He suggests using a copper or aluminum shield for the microcontroller. Right now, I am working on it.

Not only that, a motor is taking 4A in full throttle while running alone. But when all 4 motors are running, each motor is taking only 2.3A in full throttle. Therefore, the total thrust is much less than 4*45 = 180g. As my drone weighs 90g, it cannot fly higher. So, I am working on it also.
